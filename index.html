About

Know us

Tsila Tours & Transfers was founded by seasoned Southern African tourism professionals and is driven by a team of destination-based Superhosts born and raised in the regions they guide. We specialise in delivering authentic local experiences, welcoming travellers to explore Southern Africa’s natural beauty, culture, and heritage with genuine insight and warm hospitality. Our name, Tsila, reflects our Superhosts’ caring, charismatic nature and intuitive approach to travel, ensuring every journey is memorable, meaningful, and well guided. We are committed to promoting true African experiences, supporting fair-trade travel, and creating lasting value by working directly with local communities. Beyond our core transfer and charter services and guided walking tours, we also design customised tours across Zimbabwe, South Africa, Zambia, and Botswana. These experiences are tailored to individual interests, schedules, and travel styles, from cultural immersion to wildlife adventure and scenic exploration. Discover Southern Africa with trusted local experts who turn every journey into a story worth telling.

Enjoy Vic falls like a local

Walking Tours

Discover the hidden gems of Victoria Falls on a tour in English with your experienced qualified local guide. The tours are an amazing opportunity to meet people, make new friends, get tips for local foods & culture, a great way to make your stay in Victoria Falls memorable. Your Superhosts have extensively travelled through Southern African and experienced culture and all its aspects.

Guided tour of Vic Falls

Join a knowledgeable local host on a professionally guided walking tour of the iconic Mosi-oa-Tunya (Victoria Falls) and the historic Victoria Falls Bridge, built in 1905. This immersive experience lasts approximately two to two and a half hours and operates year-round, weather permitting, with advance reservations required. The walk takes guests through the rainforest, where your host shares insights into the history of the Falls, as well as the surrounding flora and fauna. The tour includes scenic viewpoints overlooking the Victoria Falls Bridge spanning the Zambezi River below the Falls and the dramatic Batoka Gorge. Guests are advised to wear comfortable walking shoes, sun protection, and warm clothing during winter months, and to bring sunscreen and mosquito repellent. This experience is ideal for first-time visitors seeking history, nature, and photography opportunities. Please email us for more information.

Township tour

Experience authentic local life on a guided Township and School or Crèche visit in Victoria Falls, led by a knowledgeable local superhost and long-time township resident. This immersive walking experience offers meaningful insight into the area’s culture, lifestyle, and history, as you explore traditional and modern housing, local markets, taverns, streets, and churches just as residents do daily. The tour provides a respectful and informative perspective on community life beyond the resort town. It concludes with a visit to a primary school or crèche, where guests learn about community education and daily challenges. Donations to the school or crèche are welcome and highly appreciated but never expected. Please note that school or crèche visits are subject to availability and operating hours. This tour offers genuine cultural exchange and understanding. Please email us for more information.

Vic Falls Town Tour

This one-hour free walking tour of Victoria Falls introduces guests to the town’s key attractions with a qualified local superhost. The guided walk covers essential highlights including the Victoria Falls Railway Station, lively craft markets, shopping areas, hotels, restaurants, and popular entertainment venues. Guests also visit the shopping and artists’ village, where they can browse specialty shops, meet local artists and craftsmen at work, and discover uniquely Zimbabwean creations. Along the way, your superhost shares valuable insights into the town’s history, present-day character, and future development plans. This relaxed walk is ideal for guests wanting orientation, local tips, and cultural context early in their stay. The tour operates daily and is suitable for all ages. Please note that any school or crèche visits are subject to availability and operating hours. Please email us for more information.

Frequently Asked Questions


Tsila Tours & Transfers does not provide car seats, but you are absolutely permitted to bring and use a size-appropriate child safety seat for child passengers. We require children age 5 and under to be secured properly in a child restraint device



Our vehicles are not fitted with wheelchair access equipment. Passengers travelling with a wheelchair are advised that they must be able to board the vehicle on foot. The wheelchair should also be collapsible.


From the time that a flight lands we anticipate that it will take approximately 30 minutes to 1 hour to clear immigration and collect baggage and to arrive at our meeting point. Any passenger who anticipates that it will take more than one hour to arrive at the meeting point should telephone our control office to inform us of the delay. Where a passenger does not alert us of a delay, we will check with the airport authorities. If we do not hear from a passenger 2 hours after a flight has landed, this will be charged as a no show.
