Know us

Tsila Transfer & Tours is a brainchild of seasoned Southern African tourism professionals. We have transformed ourselves to a united bunch of destination-based hosts, who immerse travellers with local destinations. We pride ourselves in providing destination-based hospitality throughout Southern Africa’s destinations including the untouched countrysides.

There is no Africa beyond our boundaries. We are your local African expert hosts, born and bred in our destinations. Book or enquire your travel needs in our destinations of speciality and let us be your African hosts, from a mere transfer to a multiple days trip in African, we make every host different. Yours your African host!

Ishmael Samuponda

Vic Falls & Chobe Host

Originally from Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe’s popular tourist resort town. He has quite a history and has worked in various facets of the travel industry and as an overland tour guide in Botswana, Namibia, South Africa, Zambia and Zimbabwe for 11 years. In 2018 he joined Tsila Tours & Transfers as our Victoria Falls, Zimbabwe & Chobe, Botswana Host. Given his solid tourism background we are proud to have him onboard.

Harare Host
