Questions

Free walking tour questions

 

Free tours have no fixed tour price. You are *free* to decide how much you can support and reward your local Superhost for the tour, based on how much it was worth for you. The free tour promotes a Fairtrade intercultural experience. Free tours are popular in major cities, and we are exhilarated to introduce them to Victoria Falls.

 

The tours explore different areas of Victoria Falls depending on your preference and the tours have different meeting points. Visit the Tours section for the meeting location details. If you still are not sure of the meeting place, email your Victoria Falls Superhost to receive further instructions.

We generally have good weather throughout the year in Victoria Falls. This allows us to Superhost you all year. If it rains during the tour, we might wait a little bit longer at the beginning or make an additional stop until the weather gets better.

Free tours are for everyone and best for individuals and small groups of friends/families up to 10 people. If you are a bigger group, it is necessary to book a private tour.

The guide will be standing at the meeting point with an orange t-shirt.

We let you make the final decision on payment for your guide if you are satisfied. We recommend US$5 per person.

Generally, a free tour starts with a short brief and introductions which allows extra time for everyone to join the tour. If you are very late and can still see your guide in an orange t-shirt you are welcome to join the tour.

We offer private tours in German but the language of free tours in English. Email us to enquire more details and availability of walking tours in German.

Each tour is designed to show you different highlights of Victoria Falls. All the areas are handpicked and undoubtedly worthy of a visit. Feel free to email us for any additional questions.

Yes, you will! Your Superhost is a local resident and interacts with many people in Victoria Falls daily. This assures you great opportunities to meet many locals during your tour.
